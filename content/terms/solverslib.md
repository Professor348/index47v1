__SolversLib__ is an updated and maintained fork of the older FTCLib, created by FTC Team __23511__, Seattle Solvers. One of SolversLib's modern features is easy integration with __Pedro Pathing__, and recently an integration with __Photon__ which helps with quick __loop times__! Check out the documentation at [docs.seattlesolvers.com](https://docs.seattlesolvers.com/).
