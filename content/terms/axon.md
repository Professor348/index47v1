__Axon Robotics__ is an FTC parts supplier known for their high-performance programmable servos. Their flagship products — the Axon MAX+ and Axon Mini — offer significantly more __torque__ and __speed__ than stock REV or goBILDA servos, with built-in programmable features like adjustable __speed profiles__ and __position feedback__. Teams use Axon servos for high-demand mechanisms like active __intakes__, wrist __joints__, and claw __pivots__ where a standard servo would __stall__ or be too slow. They've become increasingly popular among competitive teams looking for a servo __upgrade__ without going full custom. To buy your own, go to [axon-robotics.com](https://axon-robotics.com/)
