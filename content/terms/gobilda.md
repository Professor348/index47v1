__goBILDA__ is an FTC parts supplier known for its robust channel-based build system using metric hardware and 8mm REX shafts on a consistent 48mm grid pattern. Their product line includes the popular [Yellow Jacket motors](https://www.gobilda.com/yellow-jacket-planetary-gear-motors/), Super Strafer chassis kits, linear slides (Viper Slides), servos, and a wide range of structural and motion components. goBILDA parts are designed to __work together__ seamlessly, which makes building mechanisms faster. Many competitive FTC teams rely on goBILDA as their primary build system because of the high part density and strong community support. To see the full library visit [gobilda.com](https://www.gobilda.com/)
