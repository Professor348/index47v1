__Onshape__ is a cloud-based CAD platform developed by [PTC](https://www.ptc.com/). It is widely used in FTC because it requires no installation, runs entirely in a web browser, and offers free education accounts. Teams use Onshape to design custom brackets, drivetrain plates, intake mechanisms, and full robot assemblies. Its built-in version control and real-time collaboration let multiple team members work on the same assembly simultaneously — making it the most popular CAD tool in the FTC community.
