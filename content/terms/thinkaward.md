---
title: Think Award
aliases: [think]
---

__Think Award__is an FTC judged award given to the team with the best engineering portfolio or notebook. Judges want to see a clear engineering design process — problem identification, brainstorming, prototyping, testing, iteration, and reflection. It's not about making it look pretty — it's about showing genuine thought behind every decision. Teams that win Think typically document their failures just as much as their successes, and show how testing data drove their design changes. This award can advance teams to the next competition level.
