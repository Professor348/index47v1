__Misumi__ is a Japanese industrial parts supplier at [misumi.com](https://us.misumi-ec.com/) that many advanced FTC teams order from. They carry precision shafts, bearings, aluminum extrusion, shoulder bolts, spacers, and other mechanical components with exact specifications and tight tolerances. What makes Misumi popular in FTC is the ability to order custom-length shafts and extrusion cut to the millimeter — so you get exactly what you need without having to cut anything yourself. Shipping can take a while, so teams usually order early in the season.
