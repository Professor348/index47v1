---
title: Robot in 3 days
aliases: [RI3D]
---

__Robot in 3 days__ is an concept for FTC and FRC teams to challenge themselves the instant a season is released, the challenge is to complete a basic robot in 3 days after the season comes out, in attempt to have as many game aspects somewhat solved in that time. This can be applied to different challenges such as Robot in 30 hours which is very common as well.
