__Live Action Role Play__ commonly known as "larp" is a bad action, regularly committed by FTC students. It means that a student is trying to act like someone that they are not, or act like they are superior in some way when they are not. Example: I am part Exodus 30030. This would be factually incorrect and trying to present yourself higher as they won the FIRST world championship for the DECODE season. Larping can have many consequences such as being socially isolated where no one will want to talk or communicate with you. Visit [urbandictionary.com](https://www.urbandictionary.com/define.php?term=LARP) for more details
