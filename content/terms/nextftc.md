__NextFTC__ is a collection of command based libraries developed by Rowan McAlpin, Davis Luxenberg, and Zach Harel. NextBindings provides complex gamepad integration, and NextControl has __PID/PIDF control__, __feedforward__, __motion profiling__, and __Kalman filters__. NextFTC is written in __Kotlin__ with __Java__ support, and has extensions to work with __PedroPathing__ and __RoadRunner__. More information and documentation can be viewed at [nextftc.dev](https://nextftc.dev/).
