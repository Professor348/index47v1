---

__Fork__ is a way for programmers to __contribute__. It is a personal copy of someone else's repository that lives under your own account. It allows you to freely __experiment__ with and __modify__ a project without affecting the original (upstream) version. A fork can be later used to make a __pull request__ for the original repository owner can later choose to accept and merge it or to decline
